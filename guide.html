A Guide to Archive of Our Own

About AO3

One of the most popular fanfiction websites is Archive of Our Own, commonly abbreviated to AO3. AO3 is an archive of fan-produced works, mainly consisting of fanfiction but also including artwork and writings about fanfiction. Since it is the fanfiction site I have the most experience with, I have provided a guide to navigating the site that I hope others will find helpful.

AO3 is free to access and use, and guests on the website are allowed to read most fics and can "like" and often comment on those they can access. Users with accounts can read all fics contained on AO3, can post, and can comment. Accounts are free, and the link to sign up for one can be found here: Sign up for AO3.

Searching for Fanfics: Tags

The primary way to search for fanfics is through filtering tags. Tags are how fanfics are sorted, and tell the reader what is contained in the fic. You can filter out or in tags, which can be very specific, to search for exactly what you want to read.
